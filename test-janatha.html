ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

Janatha Fishnet, a proud part of the Janatha Group, is committed to empowering communities through quality craftsmanship and sustainable practices. Operating from our state-of-the-art 15-acre facility in Dabekatte, Udupi District, Karnataka, we manufacture premium fishing nets designed for durability, efficiency, and performance. With over 70% of our workforce composed of skilled women, we foster inclusivity and economic growth. Our meticulous production process ensures every net meets the highest quality standards through automated and manual checks. We prioritize eco-friendly solutions, reducing environmental impact while promoting responsible fishing.

At Janatha Fishnet, we don’t just deliver products—we build trust, reliability, and lasting partnerships in the global fishing industry.

PRODUCTS

Fishing Nets

HDPE Fishnets

High-Density Polyethylene fishnets are ideal for commercial deep-sea fishing, offering strength, durability, and resistance to harsh marine conditions.

  • Resistant to saltwater corrosion, ensuring long-lasting durability.
  • UV resistant, maintaining strength and performance even under direct sunlight.
  • Lightweight yet strong, reducing strain on the crew and improving operational efficiency.
  • Requires minimal maintenance, saving both time and costs.

Nylon Multifilament Fishnets

Nylon multifilament fishnets are made from high-quality nylon fibers, offering strength, flexibility, and durability.

  • Nylon multifilament fishnets excel in trawling operations, offering excellent durability and performance in deep-sea fishing.
  • Perfect for gillnetting, the nylon material minimizes entanglement and ensures reliable fish capture in varying water conditions.
  • Designed for purse seine fishing, these nets allow for efficient capture of fish species at the water’s surface.
  • Nylon multifilament nets minimize harm to fish, making them ideal for catch-and-release or harvesting high-value species.

Nylon Mono-filament Fishnets

With fewer knots than multifilament nets, they reduce drag and resist entanglement, making them ideal for precision fishing in commercial applications.

  • Highly effective in gillnetting, offering a smoother surface that reduces drag and minimizes fish escape.
  • The smooth design reduces resistance in the water, making these nets ideal for efficient trawling operations.
  • With their low-visibility design, these nets are perfect for seine fishing, allowing for efficient capture of fish without disturbing the surrounding environment.
  • Suitable for both shallow and deep waters, providing flexibility in diverse fishing environments.

Floats

DS2 Floats

DS2 Floats are durable, high-quality floating devices designed for maximum buoyancy in fishing nets, ensuring long-lasting performance in saltwater and other aquatic environments.

  • DS2 Floats provide optimal buoyancy for gillnets, ensuring proper net positioning for effective fish capture in varying water depths.
  • Perfect for seine nets, these floats maintain stability and buoyancy, allowing for smooth deployment and retrieval in both shallow and deep waters.
  • DS2 Floats offer reliable support for trawl nets, keeping them properly positioned in the water for maximum catch efficiency.
  • Their lightweight design makes handling easy, while their durability ensures long-term use in challenging marine environments.

PVC Floats

Our PVC floats are durable, lightweight, and corrosion-resistant, making them ideal for a wide range of aquatic applications.

  • PVC floats provide reliable buoyancy and stability for floating docks, ensuring they remain stable and functional in various water conditions.
  • Perfect for marina platforms, due to their durability and resistance to saltwater corrosion.
  • PVC floats are commonly used in navigation buoys, offering buoyancy and visibility for safe passage in water bodies.
  • PVC floats are a reliable choice for emergency flotation devices, ensuring safety and stability in critical situations.

Twines

Twine

Twine is a strong, flexible cord made from various materials, including cotton, jute, nylon, and polypropylene. The quality of twine depends on its material, thickness, and construction (e.g., braided or twisted).

  • Used to make fish traps, twine’s strength ensures that traps maintain their shape and function even under the weight of caught fish.
  • Twine plays a critical role in securing fish pens in aquaculture, providing strong and reliable support for aquatic farming operations.
  • Twine is used in buoy ropes to mark and anchor fishing gear, ensuring nets and equipment stay in place during operations.
  • Available in multiple materials (cotton, jute, nylon, polypropylene), twine can be adapted to different fishing environments and tasks.
