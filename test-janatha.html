ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

Janatha Fishnet, a proud part of the Janatha Group, is committed to empowering communities through quality craftsmanship and sustainable practices. Operating from our state-of-the-art 15-acre facility in Dabekatte, Udupi District, Karnataka, we manufacture premium fishing nets designed for durability, efficiency, and performance. With over 70% of our workforce composed of skilled women, we foster inclusivity and economic growth. Our meticulous production process ensures every net meets the highest quality standards through automated and manual checks. We prioritize eco-friendly solutions, reducing environmental impact while promoting responsible fishing.

At Janatha Fishnet, we don’t just deliver products—we build trust, reliability, and lasting partnerships in the global fishing industry.
